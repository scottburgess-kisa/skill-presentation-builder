### Corporate Style Guidelines

**Colours:**
- Primary: Corporate Blue (`#003D7A`)
- Secondary: Light Blue (`#4A90E2`)
- Accent: Success Green (`#28A745`), Warning Orange (`#FFC107`)
- Neutral: White (`#FFFFFF`), Light Grey (`#F8F9FA`)

**Typography:**
- Titles: Helvetica Bold 18-24 pt
- Labels: Helvetica Regular 12-14 pt
- Data: Helvetica Bold 14-16 pt
- Clean, business-appropriate styling
